  • Your resume should ideally be between 1 and 2 pages. If you have under 8 years of professional experience, try to keep it to one page. If you have more experience, a two-page resume may be appropriate.

  • Make sure to include a skills section, as it is very important. This section becomes especially useful if the employer uses an ATS (Applicant Tracking System).

  • Unless the employer or job posting states otherwise, submit your resume in PDF format. If you're using Google Docs, you can convert it by going to File → Download → PDF.

  • For each position, write around 2–5 bullet points depending on how long you worked there and how relevant the role is to the job you’re applying for.

  • Your most recent job should usually contain the most bullet points, particularly if it is closely related to the role you are applying for.

  • Describe your current role using the present continuous tense, and describe past roles using the past simple tense.
